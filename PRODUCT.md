# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a focused, low-friction way to track calories and understand
their daily nutrition without navigating a complicated fitness platform.

## Product Purpose

Kcal Count makes daily calorie tracking fast, legible, and dependable. Success
means users can record and review their nutrition with minimal interruption.

## Brand Personality

Premium, precise, restrained. The experience should feel considered and
confident, with the quiet clarity associated with Apple's product interfaces.

## Anti-references

Avoid loud fitness branding, gamified pressure, dense dashboards, decorative
gradients, excessive cards, and generic SaaS auth screens.

## Design Principles

1. Remove friction before adding features.
2. Make the next action unmistakable.
3. Use restraint to communicate confidence.
4. Keep health information calm and non-judgmental.
5. Prefer familiar, accessible controls over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, clear error
feedback, sufficient contrast, reduced motion, and mobile use by default.
